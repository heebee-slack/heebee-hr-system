Salary Structure — AI Auto-Calculated

Salary structures are now auto-calculated during hiring based on Government Parameters above.

HOW SALARY IS CALCULATED
1. HR enters: Category (Unskilled/Semi-Skilled/Skilled/Highly Skilled) + Desired Total Salary
2. AI calculates:
• Basic: Locked at govt minimum for category (cannot go below)
• HRA: Fixed 20% of Basic
• Meal Allowance: Editable (included in allowances)
• Dearance Allowance: Editable (included in allowances)
• Special Allowance: Auto-calculated remainder
3. Validation: All guardrails checked (50% limit, 40% HRA cap, etc.)
4. Result: ✓ COMPLIANT or ✗ NON-COMPLIANT with specific errors
SALARY STRUCTURE GUARANTEES
✓ Basic ≥ Government minimum (cannot go below)
✓ Basic ≥ 50% of gross salary (Code on Wages)
✓ HRA ≤ 40% of basic (tax exemption limit)
✓ All allowances ≤ 50% of gross (Code on Wages)
✓ Special allowance < 2× basic (fraud detection)
✓ 100% compliant with Punjab labor law
EXAMPLE: Head Cook at ₹28,000 (Skilled Category)
Basic: ₹15,414 (55% min for Skilled) | HRA: ₹3,083 (20%)
Meal: ₹1,500 | DA: ₹500 | Spl Allow: ₹7,503
Total Allowances: 45% of salary ✓ (within 50% limit)
📋 To hire a new employee: Go to Onboarding → Select candidate → "Mark as Hired" → Enter category + desired salary → Click "🤖 AI Calculate" → Confirm
